About Meta

Meta Platforms is an American technology company that focuses on social media, virtual reality, and digital communication technologies. The company was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg along with Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes.

Meta is widely known for creating popular social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. In 2021, the company changed its name from Facebook Inc. to Meta Platforms to focus on building the metaverse, a virtual digital world where people can interact using virtual reality and augmented reality technologies.

Today, Meta is one of the largest technology companies in the world and plays a major role in connecting billions of people through its platforms.

Meta Headquarters

Meta’s global headquarters is located in Menlo Park, California, United States.

The main campus is known as Meta Headquarters (Menlo Park Campus). The campus includes modern office buildings, research labs, and collaboration spaces for engineers and designers.

The headquarters is where many of Meta’s major products and technologies are developed, including social media platforms, virtual reality systems, and AI technologies.

Founders

Meta was originally founded as Facebook in 2004 by a group of students from Harvard University.

Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg is the co-founder and CEO of Meta. He created Facebook while studying at Harvard and later expanded the company into a global technology giant focusing on social media and the metaverse.

Eduardo Saverin

Eduardo Saverin was one of the early co-founders and helped finance the early development of Facebook.

Dustin Moskovitz

Dustin Moskovitz helped develop the early infrastructure of Facebook and played an important role in its growth.

Chris Hughes

Chris Hughes worked on product development and communication during Facebook’s early years.

Andrew McCollum

Andrew McCollum helped design the original Facebook website interface and logo.

Major Products

Meta develops many platforms and technologies used by billions of people around the world.

1. Facebook

Facebook is one of the largest social networking platforms where people connect, share posts, photos, and communicate with friends and communities.

2. Instagram

Instagram is a popular platform for sharing photos, videos, reels, and stories.

3. WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a messaging platform used globally for instant messaging, voice calls, and video calls.

4. Meta Quest

Meta Quest is a virtual reality headset used for VR gaming, virtual meetings, and immersive experiences.

5. Horizon Worlds

Horizon Worlds is Meta’s virtual reality platform where users can explore and interact in virtual environments.
