Infosys

Introduction

Infosys is one of the leading information technology companies in the world. It is an Indian multinational company that provides IT services, consulting, and digital solutions to businesses. Infosys helps organizations improve their technology systems and digital services.

History

Infosys was founded in 1981 by N. R. Narayana Murthy and six other engineers. The company started with a small investment but gradually grew into one of the largest IT companies in the world. Today Infosys is known for innovation and digital transformation.

Basic Information

  • Founded: 1981
  • Founder: N. R. Narayana Murthy
  • Headquarters: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
  • CEO: Salil Parekh
  • Employees: More than 300,000 employees worldwide

Services Provided by Infosys

  • Software Development
  • IT Consulting
  • Cloud Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cyber Security
  • Data Analytics
  • Enterprise Solutions
  • Digital Transformation Services

Global Presence

Infosys operates in more than 50 countries and has offices in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. The company works with many international organizations in industries such as banking, healthcare, retail, and telecommunications.
