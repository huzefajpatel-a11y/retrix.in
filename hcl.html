About HCL

HCL Technologies is a leading global IT services and consulting company that helps businesses in digital transformation.

HCL was founded in 1976 by Shiv Nadar. It provides software services, IT consulting, cloud computing, and engineering solutions.

Headquartered in Noida, India, HCL offers many technology products and services such as:

  • IT Consulting
  • Cloud Services
  • Cyber Security
  • Engineering Services

Key HCL Business Divisions

HCL organizes its operations into several business segments:

  • Digital Business Services
  • Engineering and R&D Services
  • Cloud and Infrastructure Services
