About KRAFTON

Krafton is a South Korean video game company known for developing and publishing popular online and mobile games. The company was originally founded in 2007 under the name Bluehole Studio and later rebranded as Krafton in 2018. It is widely recognized for creating the globally successful battle-royale game PUBG: Battlegrounds.

Over the years, Krafton has grown into one of the leading companies in the gaming industry. Its games are played by millions of players around the world on PC, console, and mobile devices. The company focuses on creating immersive gaming experiences using advanced graphics, game engines, and online multiplayer technologies.

KRAFTON Headquarters

Krafton’s global headquarters is located in Seoul, South Korea. The company operates from modern office buildings designed to support game development, creative design, and global publishing operations.

The headquarters hosts teams of game developers, designers, programmers, and artists who work together to create new games and improve existing titles. Krafton also manages multiple game development studios across the world.

Founders

Krafton was founded by Chang Byung-gyu, a South Korean entrepreneur and technology investor. He established the company with the goal of creating innovative online games and building a global gaming brand.

Chang Byung-gyu

Chang Byung-gyu helped grow the company into a major gaming publisher by supporting creative development teams and expanding Krafton’s presence in international gaming markets.

Major Games / Products

1. PUBG: Battlegrounds

PUBG: Battlegrounds is Krafton’s most famous game. It is a multiplayer battle-royale game where players compete to be the last person or team standing. The game became one of the most popular online games in the world and helped define the battle-royale genre.

2. PUBG Mobile

PUBG Mobile is the mobile version of PUBG designed for smartphones and tablets. It offers the same battle-royale experience with optimized controls and graphics for mobile devices.

3. The Callisto Protocol

The Callisto Protocol is a survival horror game published by Krafton. It features a story-driven experience set in a futuristic prison on Jupiter’s moon Callisto.

4. New State Mobile

New State Mobile is another battle-royale game developed by Krafton that features futuristic weapons, vehicles, and gameplay mechanics.
